ABOUT ME

Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Penn State. I do research under the supervision of Prof. Mahmut Kandemir and Prof. Abutalib Aghayev. My research interests mainly cover storage and file systems. Recently, we are working on zoned namespace SSD.

Before I joined Penn State, I obtained my M.E. degree from Xiamen University (advised by Prof. Suzhen Wu), and B.E. degree from Zhejiang Sci-Tech University.
